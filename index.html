Developers Student Club - by Google Developers

About: Developer Student Clubs or DSC is a University Program kickstarted by Google Developers to train thousands of student developers globally and work with their communities to solve real-life problems.

DSC IIT Bhilai was set-up primarily to ensure that we can bridge the divide between a student’s eagerness to learn and the technology evolving every day. The Developer Student Clubs (DSC) program is a grassroots channel through which the members of the community provide development skills for students, towards employability. In addition to workshops, DSCs also provide an opportunity for students to apply their newly gained skills to develop solutions for local organizations and then provide visibility via showcases.We will be taking up projects and any individual, group or organization with a good idea for a project and a thought to create an impact by making lives of people better will be welcome. We welcome all the development projects that involve the latest technologies like App development, Machine Learning, Web Development, Cloud Development and much more.

Electromos

Co-ordinator: Rithvika Pervala

About: Electromos is the electronics club of IIT Bhilai and has been the most active club in IIT Bhilai. The club organises various workshops and events to motivate students to bring their innovative ideas to real-life projects. Hands-on experience with Arduino , Raspberry Pi and various cool projects attract students of all the disciplines. Students can get electrical components from the club to build their own projects and also provide support ,guidance and ways to help students realise their conceptual designs to working models. The Club also organises many activities like seminars, exhibitions, webinars and a hackathon. Objectives and Ideas: Group of students sharing a common interest in Electronics and its Applications in engineering. Give a taste of building machines and making life easier through technology to the students of all the branches. Passion to combine electronic parts into machines and robots. Expose students to hands-on experience early in life. Promotion of Innovative ideas and designs. Inculcating the good habits and practices of engineering things.

Space Exploration Society (SES)

Co-ordinator:Ansh Gangwar and Sudeep Ranjan Sahoo

SES was started for conducting activities in observational astronomy, astrophysics, and aerodynamics. Subsequently, it was divided into 2 separate divisions to give justice to both of them under one roof - Aeromodelling and Astronomy & Astrophysics. The Astronomy and Astrophysics division specializes in astrophotography, observational astronomy, documentary watching sessions and all-round discussions on everything Space! The Aeromodelling division involves the designing, building and flying of miniaturized aircrafts, a hobby enjoyed widely by aviation enthusiasts.

DSAI

Co-ordinator: Aneesh Tickoo and Vishesh Thakur

The majority of new ventures involve significant data analysis. This data is being generated at an incredible rate. Over the last few years, hackathons and other data science competitions have increased. In this situation, we can't afford to fall behind. As a result, the DSAI Club was established at IIT Bhilai to accelerate the data science culture. This club's primary goal is to promote two fields: data science and artificial intelligence. The club will work on projects that will encourage more individuals to join the bandwagon of innovation. The club will also endeavor to develop a research group to solve problems, publish articles, and contribute to the student research culture at IIT Bhilai. .
